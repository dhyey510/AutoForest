ABOUT US

Our story begun in 2016, to be a meaningful part in Automobile Industry. Since the seeds of E-mobility were sowed globally to rehabilitate the world which is majorly affected by pollution from depletable resources, we started to make an effort worthy to be part of an era of Electric vehicles. We as AUTO FOREST, which reflects need to rescue forests by transmutation of source of energy to renewable one.

In distribution, Auto Forest concentrates in promoting EV products through a wide number of its retail network across the country. EV parts that we deal in are made by professional International and Domestic manufacturer-partners, who are carefully supervising the product quality and development.

Moreover, we are having an edge over other companies owing to our OEM facility as per which they are free to sell our spare parts under their own brand name.

OUR MISSION

Fruitful bestowal to society uplifting towards pollution free environment by creating transportation system facilitated with ease and joy.

TRANSFORMING INTO GREEN ENERGY

The fact that world needs to change whole system of transportation because of high pollution emitting vehicles have already led to depletion of natural air and also to have transportation system majorly based on sustainable source of energy for future as compared to current non-renewable sources (i.e petrol, diesel etc). Our firm foresees personal growth because of high demand of E-vehicles together with helping world to save nature from destruction.
