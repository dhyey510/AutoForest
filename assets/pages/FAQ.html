FAQs

What is E-bicycle?

E-bicycle is battery operated bicycle. It has motor at rear wheel, which is propelled by the battery power. Battery needs to get recharge with external power source once the battery is discharge.

How does it work?

It has 3 riding modes Manual is like the conventional bicycle Automatic is like a gear less scooters. Hybrid is combination of human and electric power.

Which battery does it have?

It has li-ion battery of 36 Volts.

What is motor power and torque?

Motor power is 250 Watts and 30Nm torque.

How many kilometres does it run per full charge.

7.8 Ah battery runs for 35* kms and 10.4 Ah battery runs for 50* kms

How long does battery take to get full charge?

It takes 4 hours to get full charge.

Is there any special requirement to be made to charge the battery?

No, conventional 5 Amp household socket is enough.

How much does it cost to get full charge?

It takes only half unit to get full charge.

What is the life cycle of battery?

Battery has life of 800 charging cycles.

Bicycle can run on manual mode once the battery gets full discharged?

Yes, if the battery gets full discharged you can ride it like conventional bicycle.

Do we get any warranty on the Auto Forest E-bicycles?

Yes, Motor has 1 year warranty and Battery has 1 year replacement warranty. No accidental or water damage will be covered in the warranty.

What is routine maintenance of the E bicycle?

Periodic chain lubrication and brake setting are routine maintenance and appropriate tyre pressure needs to maintain in order to ensure the range per charge.

How fast the E-bike can go?

Maximum speed of the E-bike is limited to 25KMPH according to RTO rules.

Does it require RTO registrations?

No, it does not require any RTO registration.

Does it require driving license?

No, it does not require driving license.

What age people can ride it?

Any of age group can ride it as it is bicycle only.

What is the material of frame?

Frame is made by steel with the international standard material property.
